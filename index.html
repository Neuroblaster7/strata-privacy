Privacy Policy — STRATA

Last updated: 21 July 2026

The short version. STRATA has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no server of its own. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information about you. Everything you create in the app — your name, your saved spots, your settings — stays on your device and is erased when you uninstall the app.

The app does use your device's location, but only on the device, to show you what is beneath you and around you. Your location is never sent to us, because there is nowhere for it to be sent.

1. Who this policy covers

This policy applies to the STRATA mobile application ("the app") for Android and iOS. The app is published by an independent developer. For any privacy question or request, contact:

Contact email: dav.frattini@gmail.com

2. What the app stores on your device

The following is saved locally on your phone, using the operating system's own secure app storage (UserDefaults on iOS, SharedPreferences on Android). None of it is transmitted anywhere.

3. Location

The app requests permission to use your device's location (approximate and/or precise). This is the app's core function: telling you what is under your feet.

  • Location is used only while you are using the app. The app does not request or use background location.
  • Your position is used on the device to centre the map and to scan the area around you.
  • Your position is never sent to the developer, and is not stored unless you deliberately save a spot.
  • You can refuse the permission and still use the app — you simply pan the map yourself instead of it finding you.
  • You can revoke the permission at any time in your device's system settings.

One honest disclosure: when you tap a point to inspect it, the coordinates of that point are sent to the public geology and map services listed in section 5, because that is how the app retrieves the data for that location. Those services receive a coordinate and your device's IP address as part of an ordinary web request. They do not receive your name or any identifier from the app.

4. What the app does not do

  • No account. There is no sign-up, no login, no email address collected.
  • No analytics or tracking. The app contains no analytics, crash-reporting, or attribution SDK of any kind.
  • No advertising. There are no ads and no advertising identifiers.
  • No selling or sharing of data. We have no data about you to sell or share.
  • No camera or photo access. The app does not request or use your camera or photo library.
  • No contacts, microphone, or file access.
  • No background activity. The app does nothing while it is closed.

5. Third-party services the app contacts

The app has no server of its own. To show maps and geology it talks directly to the following public services. When your device requests data from them, they necessarily receive the map coordinates being requested and your IP address, under their own privacy policies. We have no agreement with them that gives us any data about you.

6. Purchases

The app offers an optional one-time paid unlock. Payment is handled entirely by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We never see or receive your payment details — no card number, no billing address, no name. The app only stores a local flag recording that the unlock is active.

7. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone. Since no personal data is collected at all, no data about a child could be collected through the app.

8. Your rights

Because the app collects no personal data and transmits none to us, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, export, or delete. If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, your rights under the GDPR (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection) are therefore satisfied by design: the data never leaves your device in the first place.

Deleting your data

All app data is local. To erase it completely you can:

  • Uninstall the app — this removes all saved spots, settings, and caches; or
  • On Android, go to Settings → Apps → STRATA → Storage → Clear data; or
  • On iOS, delete the app, or use Settings → General → iPhone Storage → STRATA.

You do not need to contact us to delete your data, and there is no server-side copy to request.

9. Security

Your data is protected by your device's own app sandboxing and, where you have enabled it, device encryption. All network requests the app makes use encrypted HTTPS connections.

10. Changes to this policy

If the app ever begins collecting data — for example if an account system or analytics were added — this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the "last updated" date at the top will change. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
